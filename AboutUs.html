About Us

Avanthi Pharma Overview

Avanthi Pharma is a dynamic and innovative pharmaceutical company committed to advancing healthcare globally. Founded in 2022 and headquartered in Hyderabad, India, Avanthi Pharma has rapidly emerged as a trusted leader in the pharmaceutical industry.

Mission and Values:

Avanthi Pharma is driven by a mission to improve patient outcomes and enhance quality of life through the development and distribution of high-quality pharmaceutical products. Our core values of innovation, integrity, quality, and accessibility guide every aspect of our operations.

Product Portfolio:

Avanthi Pharma offers a diverse range of pharmaceutical products across various therapeutic areas, including but not limited to cardiology, oncology, neurology, gastroenterology, and dermatology. Our portfolio includes both generic and branded medications, developed with a focus on efficacy, safety, and patient satisfaction.

Quality Assurance:

At Avanthi Pharma, quality is of utmost importance. We adhere to stringent quality control measures and regulatory standards to ensure the safety, efficacy, and compliance of our products. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are equipped with the latest technology and undergo regular inspections to maintain the highest levels of quality assurance.

Community Engagement:

Avanthi Pharma is committed to giving back to the communities we serve. Through various social responsibility initiatives and philanthropic efforts, we strive to make a positive impact on healthcare access, education, and infrastructure in underserved areas.

Future Outlook:

As we continue to grow and expand our presence in the global pharmaceutical market, Avanthi Pharma remains dedicated to advancing healthcare through innovation, quality, and collaboration. We are committed to addressing unmet medical needs, improving patient outcomes, and contributing to a healthier future for all.

With a focus on excellence and a passion for innovation, Avanthi Pharma is poised to make significant contributions to the advancement of healthcare worldwide.

Avanthi Pharma is committed towards “not just healthcare but lifecare.”

Key Peoples

Ganesh

Founder & CEO

Ganesh is an Executive, whole time Director and the Chief Executive Officer of our Company. He has been a Director of our Company since January 21, 2020. Ganesh holds a bachelor's degree in Pharmaceutical Sciences from Andhra University. He brings extensive experience from his tenure at Pfizer, where he served in various roles, including research and development, from 1995 to 2019.

Mark Angela

MD

Mark Angela serves as the Managing Director of our Company. She has been a Director since January 21, 2020. Mark Angela holds a bachelors and masters degree in Science from Andhra University. She was also a research scholar at the College of Science and Technology, Andhra University from 1985 to 1992 and went on to obtain her Ph.D. in the year 1992.

Britney D'Souza

Marketing Chief

Britney D'Souza is the visionary leader behind our marketing strategies. With a wealth of experience and a keen eye for market trends, she drives our company's marketing initiatives to new heights. Britney has been an integral part of our team since January 21, 2020, bringing her expertise in strategic planning and brand management. She holds a bachelors and masters degree in Science from Texas University and completed her Ph.D. in Marketing in 1992 after conducting groundbreaking research during her time as a research scholar at the College of Science and Technology, Texas State University from 1985 to 1992.
